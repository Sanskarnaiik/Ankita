Moments

Some moments with you didn’t pass, they settled quietly into my heart.
Time felt softer when it was spent with you, like it knew it was home.
There were moments with you that changed me without saying a word.
Not every memory was loud, but every one of them mattered.
With you, even the ordinary moments felt worth remembering.
Some moments stay, long after the moment itself is gone.
